Brent Faiyaz

Biography

Brent Faiyaz, born Christopher Brent Wood, is a contemporary R&B singer, songwriter, and producer whose sly vocals, frequently applied to atmospheric slow jam productions, resemble a distinctive cross between Slim (of 112) and the Weeknd. Active since the mid-2010s as a solo artist and as one-third of Sonder, he made a major commercial breakthrough with a featured appearance on GoldLink's multi-platinum, Grammy-nominated "Crew" (2016).

Born in Columbia, Maryland on September 19, 1995, Brent Faiyaz started making music at the age of 12. He has been noted saying that he draws inspiration from rappers like Curren$y and Lil Wayne, but his musical interests range from Frank Sinatra to Marvin Gaye. He eventually started uploading his music to SoundCloud. A couple years later he settled in Los Angeles to further his career.
